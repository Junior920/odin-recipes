CHICKEN

Description

This is a simple and delicious chicken recipe that is perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place the chicken breasts on a baking sheet.
  3. Drizzle olive oil over the chicken.
  4. Season with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika.
  5. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through.
  6. Let it rest for 5 minutes before serving.
