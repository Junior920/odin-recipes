Burger

Description

This is a simple and delicious burger recipe that is perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 4 burger buns
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 slices of cheese (optional)
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat a grill or skillet to medium-high heat.
  2. Form the ground beef into 4 equal patties.
  3. Season both sides of the patties with salt and pepper.
  4. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness.
  5. If using cheese, place a slice on each patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.
  6. Toast the burger buns on the grill or skillet for about 1 minute.
  7. Assemble the burgers with your favorite toppings and serve.
